Overview

Purpose

This website will showcase and sell a curated selection of tech and gaming products. It aims to provide a visually appealing and user-friendly shopping experience.

Audience

The target audience includes tech enthusiasts, hobbyists, gamers, pc builders, and anyone looking for unique and cool tech products.

Dynamic elements

I will use JavaScript to create an interactive product gallery, add a shopping cart feature that stores the product id so it can be used in the cart, and add a search feature to filter through different products that the user might be looking for.

Wireframes

Child page Wireframe:

Home

On the home page there will be four products at a time on display. I will add 8 products total that can be searched for and added to cart. Normally I wouldnt have the add to cart on the main page but I added it here so that users can easily add it I will use the search bar so that the products in my inventory can be searched for. It stores the product Id and adds a number to the cart so that the user knows how much has been stored in the cart.

Cart (child page)

This page is pretty straightforward. It takes the product Id's that have been added to the cart and displays a simplified version of the product overview. The middle will calculate the price, then the right side will be where the user fills in their information so that they can get the product shipped to them and pay for it.

About Us

TechShoppin began as a small tech company in Bakersfield, California, driven by a passion for innovation and a love for all things tech. Our journey started with a small storefront, where we provided gadgets and tech to our local community. As our reputation for quality products and exceptional customer service grew, so did our ambition. Today, we have expanded our reach by selling tech online, allowing us to connect with tech enthusiasts, gamers, and hobbyists across the globe. At TechShoppin, we are committed to bringing you the best in technology, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience.
