About Us

TechShoppin began as a small tech company in Bakersfield, California, driven by a passion for innovation and a love for all things tech. Our journey started with a small storefront, where we provided gadgets and tech to our local community. As our reputation for quality products and exceptional customer service grew, so did our ambition. Today, we have expanded our reach by selling tech online, allowing us to connect with tech enthusiasts, gamers, and hobbyists across the globe. At TechShoppin, we are committed to bringing you the best in technology, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience.
